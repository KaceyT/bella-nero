Bella + Nero

Bella and Nero were the most beloved beings on the earth to me. Perfect souls just full of love, inquisitiveness, and a playful spirit. To me, they were not merely my pets; they truly were my best friends. It sounds crazy, but I really believe they were able to understand what I was thinking and feeling, even though we couldn't speak to each other. Their loss has been the greatest of my life, and I don't think I'll every fully get over it. I think about them each and every day. This is a site to not only practice my coding skills, but to pay tribute to the loves of my life.
